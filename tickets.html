Vampire Ride

Just like a bat out of hell, this family-friendly rollercoaster (height restriction only 1.1 metres) gets you soaring through the skies and flying over the treetops with your legs dangling below you. It’s fang-tastic and once bitten, you’ll want to fly again and again.

Just Washed Teacups

The whole system consists of 3 different spinning platforms. The big main platform carries 4 smaller platforms and rotates with about 8 rpm. Each small platform comes with 3 cars that are able to spin free. So let’s hop in and swirl and twirl.

Into the Future

You’ll be screaming with laughter whilst being greeted by aliens and other exciting chraters from the future. In this ride, you will have to fight the past as you are flying away into the future. Hurry up and catch the last ride to the futre before its too late!
